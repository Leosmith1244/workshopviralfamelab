About the Workshop

Unlocking the secrets of shareable content for maximum impact.

In today's fast-paced digital world, getting your message seen and shared is harder than ever. The "Viral Fame Lab" workshop is designed specifically for PR Pros Toastmasters members who want to cut through the noise and create content that truly resonates.

We'll dive deep into Aristotle's timeless Rhetorical Triangle—Ethos (credibility), Pathos (emotion), and Logos (logic)—and show you how to apply these powerful persuasion techniques to your club's promotional content, making it not just seen, but truly contagious.

What You'll Learn:

  • Hooking audiences in 3 seconds.
  • Triggering emotional responses that drive shares.
  • Crafting irresistible, ethical clickbait headlines.
  • Leveraging trending topics for maximum reach.
