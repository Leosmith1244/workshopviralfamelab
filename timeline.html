Workshop Agenda & Timeline

A detailed breakdown of our 90-minute journey to viral content mastery.

0:00–0:10

Member Introductions & Check-In

Facilitator: Toastmaster of the Day

Activity: Each member shares their name, one goal for creating viral content, and a sentence using the Word of the Day ("Contagious").

Cues for TMoD:

  • Start Strong: "Welcome, future viral content creators! Let's kick off with quick intros. Share your name, one goal for today, and a sentence using our Word of the Day: 'Contagious'!"
  • Keep it Moving: "Fantastic! Remember, 30 seconds max per person to keep us on track."
  • Energy Check: "Love the enthusiasm! That's exactly the 'contagious' vibe we're aiming for today."
1
0:10–0:18

Speech 1: "The 3-Second Rule"

Speaker: Club VP of Public Relations

Objective: Explain why social media content gets skipped in 3 seconds and how to hook audiences using Aristotle’s Rhetorical Triangle.

Cues for TMoD:

  • Introduction: "Get ready to learn why your brilliant posts might be getting scrolled past in the blink of an eye. Our VP of PR is here to arm you with the ultimate '3-Second Rule'!"
  • Transition: "Thank you, [Speaker's Name]! Now, let's put that rule into action with our first interactive activity!"
2
0:18–0:22

Activity 1: Live TikTok Critique

Facilitator: Speech Evaluator

Activity: Critique 2-3 pre-selected TikTok posts in Zoom chat (hook vs. flop, linking to Ethos, Pathos, Logos).

Cues for Evaluator:

  • Set the Scene: "Alright, your mission, should you choose to accept it: tell us in the chat, in 30 seconds, why these TikToks are viral... or not! Ready, set, critique!"
  • Summarize: "Fantastic analysis, everyone. [Summarize key takeaways, reinforcing Ethos, Pathos, Logos]."
3
0:22–0:30

Speech 2: "Dopamine Hacks"

Speaker: Club President

Objective: Teach how to trigger shares with outrage, awe, or FOMO using the Rhetorical Triangle.

Cues for TMoD:

  • Introduction: "Ever wonder why some content just explodes online? Our Club President is about to reveal the psychological triggers that make posts irresistible – prepare for 'Dopamine Hacks'!"
  • Transition: "Incredible! Now that your brains are buzzing with those hacks, let’s get creative and rewrite some dull posts!"

0:30–0:34

Activity 2: Rewrite a Dull Post

Facilitator: Speech Evaluator

Activity: Groups in breakout rooms rewrite a bland club post to include outrage, awe, or FOMO. Share in chat.

Cues for Evaluator:

  • Brief Breakout: "Head to your breakout rooms! You have 2 minutes to transform this dull post into a dopamine-triggering masterpiece. Go!"
  • Highlight & Explain: "And the most contagious rewrite goes to… [Group/Person]! Why did this one hit home? [Explain emotional appeal]."

0:34–0:42

Speech 3: "The Dark Arts of Clickbait"

Speaker: Club Secretary

Objective: Share ethical clickbait techniques for irresistible Toastmasters headlines.

Cues for TMoD:

  • Introduction: "Ready to master the art of the irresistible headline, without losing your soul? Our Club Secretary will unveil 'The Dark Arts of Clickbait' – ethically, of course!"
  • Transition: "Powerful insights! Let's now put your new headline skills to the test with a challenge that might just create our next viral sensation!"

0:42–0:46

Activity 3: Create a Scandalous-But-True TM Headline

Facilitator: Speech Evaluator

Activity: Members write ethical clickbait headlines for a club event in Zoom chat. Poll for the most contagious.

Cues for Evaluator:

  • Prompt: "Your challenge: create a 'scandalous-but-true' headline for a Toastmasters event! Post it in the chat! Make it ethically irresistible."
  • Celebrate: "And the winner is... [Headline]! That's pure genius – it sparks curiosity and promises value!"

Speech 4: "Trend-Jacking Olympics"

Speaker: Club VP of Education

Objective: Show how to leverage trending memes or topics for Toastmasters promotion.

Cues for TMoD:

  • Introduction: "Ever wished your club could ride the wave of the next big meme? Our VP of Education is here to show us how to win the 'Trend-Jacking Olympics'!"

0:54–0:58

Activity 4: Meme Rebrand Race

Facilitator: Speech Evaluator

Activity: Teams in breakout rooms rebrand a trending meme for club promotion (text description or Canva link). Share in chat.

Cues for Evaluator:

  • Quick Challenge: "Breakout time! Your team's mission: take a trending meme and make it work for PR Pros Toastmasters. Be bold, be funny, be contagious!"
  • Share & Laugh: "Let's see those meme masterpieces! [Share top submissions] – these are brilliant! I think we just found our next club promotion!"

0:58–1:08

Debrief & Call to Action

Facilitator: Toastmaster of the Day

Activity: Members share one actionable step they’ll take to create viral club content (Zoom chat or unmute, 30 seconds each).

Cues for TMoD:

  • Reflect: "Wow, what an intense lab! Let's bring it home. In the chat, or unmute quickly: what's ONE 'contagious' action you're taking from today?"

Club Business & Closing
